PLANTS AND ANIMALS LIVING IN THE INDIAN OCEAN


Surprising to some, not all things living in the water are the same regionally!!
Here is some information about PlantsThe Indian Ocean has some unique animals living in it, but also some traditional animals seen all across the ocean.
Some main plants living in the region are eelgrass, phytoplankton, seaweed, kelp, and algae.Here is a source talking more about the plants



Here is some information about Animals
Animals in the ocean are abundant, and there is quite a variety of them. Scientists do not even know of all the animals that exist throughout the ocean. Some well known animals in the indian ocean are blue whales, dolphins, great white sharks, green sea turtles, humpback whales, seahorse, dugongs (also reffered to as an ocean cow), and sperm whales. More information on these animals here



Some animals or plants in the Indian Ocean are more dangerous than others..

What are these animals you may ask? They vary from the traditional great white sharks which have a reputation for being scary to box jellyfish to the more unknown Barringtonia asiatica. Barringtonia asiatica is a tree, which all parts are poisonous upon consumption. Small powder of this plant can be enough to stun or even kill fish
