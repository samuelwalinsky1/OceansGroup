CULTURE SURROUNDING THE INDIAN OCEAN


Culture is different all around the world! We should take time to understand it no matter where it is. The Indian Ocean is located between Africa and Austral-Asia and the Southern Ocean. There are many popular islands, including places like Mauritius, Seychelles, Reunion, Madagascar, Maldives, and Sri Lanka. These places all have unique practices and culture.



Waterways
Places for water and ships to pass are essential for smaller communities throughout this huge ocean. The Suez Canal in Egypt, and the Strait of Malacca between Malaysia and Indonesia are the two most well known waterways in the Indian Ocean.
