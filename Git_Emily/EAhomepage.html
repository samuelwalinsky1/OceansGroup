INDIAN OCEAN HOMEPAGE



This is a page about the Indian Ocean!
The Indian Ocean is located between Africa and Austral-Asia and the Southern Ocean. It covers 14.4% of the earths total surface! The lowest point is the Java Trench, thats 7,258 metres (23,812ft) deep. The average depth is about 3,890 metres (12,762 ft).

Here are some fun facts!

The temperature depends on location and the ocean's currents. The nearer to the Equator the warm the water tends to be. 28 degrees Celsius or 82 degrees Fahrenheit or higher is reached in coastal regions near the equator.
On average the Indian Ocean has a minimum temperature of 22 degrees Celsius/71 degree Fahrenheit. However in the southern regions, temperatures drop quickly below 40 degrees latitude south.
