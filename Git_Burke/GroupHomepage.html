71% of Earths surface is covered in water and 96% of this water is held in its oceans. The four oceans on Earth are: Pacific, Atlantic, Indian, and Arctic. Each of these oceans vary in surrounding culture, climate, animal and plant life, and dozens of other unique factors that are entirely dependent on the ocean. This is why we chose the oceans as our topic of choice for this assignment. Although they all share common ground in that they're all oceans, some of them couldn't be more different from another.
