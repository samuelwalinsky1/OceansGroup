Group Homepage       Human Travel       Plants and Animals       Hurricanes

The Atlantic Ocean is the second largest ocean on Earth and rests on the borders of five continents and over a hundred countries. I chose the Atlantic as I believe it tells an interesting story of the best our planet has to offer. Whether it's the cultures of its islands, the feats of human exploration, or the extensive sea-life unique to this ocean region, there is no shortage of excitement with the Atlantic Ocean.

                 

This webpage will be split into five of the most compelling topics related to the Atlantic Ocean. First will be a look at the exploration and travel that took place over the ocean. This will include looks at the Titanic, Amelia Erhardt, and the discovery of the new world. Our second linked page will include material on the plants and animals of the Atlantic, and our third page will look at the cultures of Atlantic Islands. Finally, our fourth and final page will go in-depth on the weather phenomenon of Hurricanes and the role that the Atlantic Ocean plays in their development.
